Why walking your dog is important?

Walking your dog a couple times a week has several health and social benefits for dog owners. Improved cardiovascular fitness, lower blood pressure, stronger muscles and bones (as a result of frequent walking), and reduced stress are just a few of the advantages. A frequent walk is also beneficial to your pet's health.
